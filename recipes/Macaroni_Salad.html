Macaroni Salad



Descriptiom

Macaroni salad is a classic, creamy side dish made with cooked elbow macaroni, fresh vegetables like carrots and bell peppers, and a tangy mayonnaise-based dressing. It’s a refreshing and satisfying dish that’s perfect for picnics, barbecues, or a light lunch. Chilled and flavorful, it combines texture and taste in every bite.

What makes macaroni salad so versatile is how easily it can be customized to suit different tastes. You can add diced eggs, pickles, shredded chicken, or even cheese for extra richness and protein. The balance of creamy dressing with the crunch of vegetables makes it both comforting and refreshing. Served cold, it's not just easy to prepare but also a great make-ahead dish for gatherings or everyday meals.

Ingredients

  • ½ (16 ounce) package macaroni
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup sour cream
  • ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1 tablespoon sweet pickle juice
  • 1 tablespoon celery seed
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • ¼ cup chopped red onion
  • ¼ cup pimento-stuffed green olives
  • ⅛ cup chopped sweet pickle
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • ½ green bell pepper, chopped

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until al dente. Drain; rinse with cold water. Set aside.

  2. Meanwhile, place eggs in a saucepan, and fill with enough cold water to cover eggs completely by about 1 inch. Bring water to a boil. Cover, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 20 minutes. Immediately remove from hot water and cool in an ice water bath. When cool enough to handle, peel and chop eggs.

  3. In a medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise, sour cream, dry mustard, sweet pickle juice, and celery seed. Season with salt and black pepper.

  4. In a large bowl, combine drained pasta, celery, onion, olives, sweet pickles, carrots, green pepper, and chopped eggs. Stir in dressing, and mix well. Refrigerate before serving.
